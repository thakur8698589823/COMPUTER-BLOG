1) Vibration mode – When someone calling you then your phone is vibrating,Keybord vibrating, SMS vibrating. This vibrating can consumed so many baterry in our phone. So turn off your vibrating mode in your phone.

2) Automaticaly sync – Well this feature is very important in your phone. It can sync automatic your photos, contact numbers. it can get backup automaticaly in your google account or google drive . But if you not use your bakup so turn off autosync mode. It can save your battery .for autosync it required internet/data in background so is use larg amount of battery as well as data also, so turn of autosync mode.

3) Brightness – If your mobile brightness is full so your mobile battery is dead very fastly and your phone is also start heating. So when use your smartphone in your home then do your mobile brightness low. It also helpfull for your Eyes.

4) Background apps – When we open some apps one time and not use till the day so this apps can use internet and battery also. so if you not required some app for use so remove these apps from your phone background.

5) WiFi – WiFi is on your phone so it can continuously search a new wifi network so it can use large amout of baterry in your phone so turn off wifi if you not required it.

6) GPS – If you not use GPS than also the Gps is on in yourphone so Gps in yourphone . so Gps use so many battery. if you not required GPS than turn off GPS

7) Wedgets / live wallpaper – Live wallpaper can use internet and larg amout of battry . So use simple wallpaper it can helpful for saving battery.
